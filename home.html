Indian Forces

The Indian Armed Forces are the military forces of the Republic of India. They consist of the Indian Army, Indian Navy, and Indian Air Force, as well as various paramilitary forces and strategic nuclear command.

  • Indian Army
  • Indian Navy
  • Indian Air Force

Indian Army

The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces. It is responsible for the defense of the country's land borders and internal security.

Indian Navy

The Indian Navy is the naval branch of the Indian Armed Forces. It is responsible for the defense of the country's maritime borders and the protection of its offshore assets.

Indian Air Force

The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. It is responsible for the defense of the country's airspace and the projection of air power in support of ground and naval operations.
